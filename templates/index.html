How To Run A 2-3 Zone

We are here to help you learn how to run a 2-3 zone for your basketball team. You should use a 2-3 zone when:

  • 1. You want to enforce better interior defense
  • 2. Increase your team's ability to rebound the ball
  • 3. Force the other team to shoot further away from the basket
